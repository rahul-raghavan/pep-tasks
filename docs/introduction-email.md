# PEP Tasks is Live!

Hi Team,

We're rolling out **PEP Tasks** — a simple tool to help us assign, track, and follow up on operational work across the school. Think of it as our internal to-do system where nothing falls through the cracks.

Here's what it does in a nutshell:

- **App Admins** create tasks and assign them to team members with a due date and priority. Teachers on the app can use it to assign various operational tasks to Harish, who will further delegate to his team.
- **Staff** see their tasks, mark them in progress, and submit them when done
- **App admins verify** completed work and apply a quick star rating
- Everyone can **add comments and attach files** to any task they're involved with
- A **dashboard** shows you what's due, what's overdue, and recent activity at a glance
- The app is **fast and lightweight** — pages load with smooth animations and only fetch what you need

## How to Get Started

1. Go to pep-tasks.vercel.app and click **Sign in with Google** using your school email
2. You'll land on your **Dashboard** — this is your home base
3. Check **Your Tasks** to see what's assigned to you
4. Click any task to see details, add comments, or update its status

That's it! No passwords to remember — just your regular Google login.

## Quick Tips

- **Staff**: When you start working on something, tap "Start Working" so your admin knows it's in progress. When you're done, hit "Mark Complete."
- **Admins**: After someone completes a task, you'll see it under "Pending Verification." Give it a star rating (1–5) and optionally leave a comment.
- **Everyone**: You'll get a browser notification when a new task is assigned to you or someone comments on your task. Make sure to allow notifications when prompted!
